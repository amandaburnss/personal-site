About me

I'm a young graphic and artist from rural Ontario. I'm familiar and comfortable in digital programs such as Illustrator, indesign and procreate. I have a love for print media and book design as well as illustration. Over the course of my three years at Niagara college I've learned many things about the industry but I'm always looking for new things to learn and try. I encourage you to take a look at my work and if you have any questions feel free to contact me.
